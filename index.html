What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. It helps determine if your weight is healthy.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30 and above

Note:

BMI doesn't differentiate between muscle and fat. Consult a health professional for a complete assessment.
